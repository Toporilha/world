These Are the Best Anti-Aging Products, According to Thousands of Reviews

If you're looking for the best anti-aging creams and products, thousands on Amazon have already done the testing for you.

By Claudia Fisher Updated February 27, 2020 Advertisement Save FB Tweet More Pinterest Email Send Text Message Print

If you want to dip your toes in the tide of anti-aging products , a good place to start is by seeing what other people have tested and liked rather than trying to sort marketing claims from fact and gimmicks from necessity. Combined, Amazon’s 12 best-selling anti-aging products have racked up over 25,000 five-star ratings, which is a decent-sized sounding board for your skincare concerns .

You’ll notice many of the anti-aging products that rank high up in Amazon’s best-selling section feature an ingredient called hyaluronic acid . If you’re unfamiliar with the substance, it’s a natural hydrator for the skin that’s particularly skilled at drawing in and holding moisture. Other familiar anti-aging go-tos that show up in Amazon’s best anti-aging products are retinol, which promotes skin cell turnover, Vitamins C and E, natural extracts, and chemical exfoliants like glycolic, salicylic, and lactic acids .

While each product is under the anti-aging umbrella, the creams, moisturizers, and serums vary in the specific aging concerns—like fine lines and wrinkles , dullness, dark spots, uneven texture, and poor elasticity—they more aggressively target. If you have particularly sensitive skin, the best practice is to read ingredient lists carefully so you can avoid common skin irritants in beauty products, like added fragrance. And because everyone’s skin is different, it’s always helpful to pay attention to the user testimonials to get a better sense of the results or potential side effects you can expect and then patch test the product on your own skin before using it all over you face and neck. Lastly, don’t get discouraged if you don’t see immediate results from one of these anti-aging products; anti-aging benefits take time and sometimes even a little trial and error to find the right product for your skin type and concerns.

Best Daily Cream: Baebody Retinol Moisturizer Cream Best Night Cream: RoC Retinol Correxion Deep Wrinkle Night Cream Best Serum for Sensitive Skin: Cosmedica Skincare Pure Hyaluronic Acid Serum Best for Skin Tightening: Eva Naturals Vitamin C Skin Clearing Serum Best Face Wash: InstaNatural Vitamin C Facial Cleanser Best Sunscreen: Elta MD UV Clear Facial Sunscreen Broad-Spectrum SPF 46 Best Neck Firming Cream: Besito Advanced Neck Cream with Peptides, Vitamin E, and Shea Butter

Without further ado, here is the full list of Amazon's best anti-aging creams, serums, moisturizers, and more, according to thousands of reviews.

Amazon

1 Baebody Retinol Moisturizer Cream

When wondering what is the best wrinkle cream on the market, Amazon shoppers will likely guide you to this number-one anti-aging best-seller. Baebody's Retinol Moisturizer Cream has an impressive review roster with nearly 3,000 five stars. A lot of customers who left positive reviews on the retinol anti-aging cream say they've noticed a reduction of fine lines, a dewier or more glowing complexion , faded dark spots, and that the face cream isn't greasy at all. The brand's other products are nothing to balk at, either. Pair this wrinkle cream with the brand's eye gel ($25; amazon.com ) to look even brighter and refreshed each morning–the dark circles eliminator has nearly 9,000 five-star reviews itself.

To buy: $20; amazon.com .

amazon.com

2 TruSkin Naturals Vitamin C Serum

Nearly 6,000 five-star reviewers swear by this bestselling anti-aging serum with powerful vitamin C and five percent hyaluronic acid, two of the most essential ingredients for preventing wrinkles, boosting collagen production, and reducing the appearance of dark spots for a more even overall skin tone. Even better, the plant-based formula means no additives or fragrances just healthy, all-natural ingredients like Aloe vera, jojoba oil, and witch hazel. The brand is so confident you’ll think its product is the best anti-aging serum that it offers a 90-day money back refund if you don’t. Store the wrinkle-fighting serum in the fridge or another cool, dark place to maintain optimal freshness.

To buy: $20; amazon.com .

amazon.com

3 Neutrogena Hydro Boost Hyaluronic Acid Face Cream

One of a few hyaluronic acid-infused products to rank as one of Amazon's best anti-aging products, Neutrogena's Hydro Boost face gel-cream is a popular water-based moisturizer that provides non-greasy hydration for an array of skin types, especially combination, extra dry, and aging. Although the gel texture absorbs quickly, the formula harnesses the power of hyaluronic acid to deeply hydrate your skin long after you've rubbed it in. One particularly enraptured reviewer commented that, in addition to making her skin feel "velvety," she experienced dramatic improvements after one single use. "Went to bed, woke up the next morning my skin looked and felt totally different. No fabrication. No exaggeration. The texture was different. I had a glow. And even my acne wasn't as red, or agitated looking. And had seemed actually slightly minimized. I began using it from that day forward and my cystic acne has ceased, my skin is supple, clear, brightened." Use the anti-wrinkle cream daily on both your face and neck after cleansing to achieve the most noticeable results.

To buy: $16 (was $19); amazon.com .

amazon.com

4 Elta MD UV Clear Facial Sunscreen Broad-Spectrum SPF 46

Sun protection is no joke when it comes to maintaining healthy, youthful-looking skin, and EltaMD Skincare’s UV Clear facial sunscreen is Amazon’s number one best-seller in the category. With nearly 90 percent of the over 4,500 customer reviews being four and five stars, shoppers call this the "best sunscreen" and "worth the money." Some have even noticed a reduction of dark spots, and the oil-free formula is especially good for those with sensitive skin prone to acne, rosacea, or discoloration. Even though the facial sunscreen's main ingredient is zinc oxide, you won't have to worry about a white cast or thick reside: Shoppers love the lightweight texture and say the cream is fast-absorbing and effective at protecting them from both UVA and UVB rays. While protecting your skin from harsh rays, the sunscreen also goes to work on your overall complexion. With niacinamide, hyaluronic acid, and lactic acid, the formula hydrates, soothes, and diminishes the appearance of age spots, hyperpigmentation, and fine lines while remaining mild on your skin.

To buy: $33 (Originally $35); amazon.com .

Amazon

5 Cosmedica Pure Hyaluronic Acid Serum

"Pure hyaluronic acid serum" sounds luxuriously anti-aging, so it's good for everyone involved that Cosmedica's product lives up to its name. Reviewers praise the anti-aging serum for its non-drying, easy-to-spread texture, skin-softening capabilities, and gentlness even on sensitive skin. Many customers use this skincare product in tandem with other anti-aging creams and face moisturizers to give their skin a more intense dose of hydration and have reported noticing plumper, dewier, and younger-looking skin after a few weeks of regular use. The serum comes in three sizes, and the price is pretty hard to beat when it comes to anti-aging serums especially one that’s 100 percent hyaluronic acid, aka the gold standard of wrinkle-fighting ingredients.

To buy: from $11; amazon.com .

Amazon

6 LilyAna Naturals Retinol Cream Moisturizer

While prescription level Vitamin-A derivatives, like the popular retinoid tretinoin , can be super drying and aren't typically recommended for daily use, retinol can be a key ingredient in your everyday anti-aging skincare routine with some effective and less harsh over-the-counter products. Happy customers hail LilyAna's anti-aging cream as a "powerful and fabulous product" that "changed the playing field." If you scroll through the review section of Amazon, you'll see several before-and-after photos showcasing improvements in people's skin tone, texture, overall color, and acne scarring. One 70-year-old reviewer even highlighted the face cream's effectiveness at reducing her "very deep wrinkles" after only three weeks of consistent use. Just remember: Always wear sunscreen when using retinol products because your skin will be more susceptible to burns.

To buy: $20; amazon.com .

amazon.com

7 Olay Regenerist Micro-Sculpting Cream with Hyaluronic Acid and Vitamin B3

Olay’s popular face moisturizer with collagen-boosting peptides has climbed up the ranks of Amazon best-sellers, earning over 3,000 perfect reviews thanks to its anti-aging formula. With hyaluronic acid and vitamin B3 (niacinamide), the cream works to plump up skin, erase fine lines and wrinkles, and regenerate surface skin cells. Some of the happy customers reported tighter, firmer skin along the jawline and on the neck and silkier, more moisturized texture. One reviewer even praised the anti-aging cream for calming her rosacea in just one week.

To buy: $22; amazon.com .

Amazon

8 Eva Naturals Skin Clearing Serum

Eva Naturals' Skin Clearing Serum uses some of the most popular dual anti-aging and acne-fighting ingredients currently in skincare products. Aside from retinol, the 2-percent dose of salicylic acid helps clear out clogged pores and exfoliate the top layer of your skin, buffing out fine lines and impurities and removing dead skin cells. The niacinamide, a popular form of topical vitamin B3, featured in the product’s formula has anti-inflammatory effects as well, helping to soothe irritated skin. This anti-aging serum has the hydrating power of hyaluronic acid, too, and brightening effects of Vitamin C. If it sounds like this product has it all, customers want you to know the anti-wrinkle serum really does. A review titled "Unexpected fast results" describes, "Have only used it a couple of times but wow this stuff is powerful! Immediate unexpected result my 11 lines and laugh lines gone. It is nongreasy and you feel skin tighten, even my pores diminished giving me an instant lift. At first the smell is not pleasant but it goes away quickly. So my bare faced skin is more radiant. I can't wait to see long term effects."

To buy: $15; amazon.com .

amazon.com

9 InstaNatural Vitamin C Cleanser

This gentle facial cleanser packs in both acne-fighting and anti-aging benefits, which is probably how it became the number-one best seller in Amazon's Facial Cleansing Gels category. While working to smooth the skin’s texture, the formula’s ingredients buff away dulling skin cells and reduce the appearance of sun spots, discoloration, and other damage from environmental stressors and exposure. The vitamin C, green tea, chamomile, and sugar cane extracts help defend the skin throughout the day, and the aloe vera and coconut water maintain a refreshed feeling and healthy complexion. Some of the five-star reviewers, of which there are nearly 2,600, noticed results in only one week and call the cleanser “perfect,” “the bomb,” and “best face wash ever.” Pair it with an exfoliating toner or serum to round out your anti-aging skincare routine.

To buy: $20; amazon.com .

Amazon

10 Tree of Life Vitamin C Serum

Vitamin C is a powerful antioxidant skincare enthusiasts love to have on hand to brighten their skin and improve overall pigmentation. Vitamin C, also called ascorbic acid, is found naturally in our skin and is integral in the process of creating and storing collagen –you know, that anti-aging superhero our bodies produce less and less of as we age. A whopping 84 percent of the over 6,400 reviews are four and five stars, with comments that the anti-aging serum helps improve discoloration, even tone, soften skin's texture, and diminish fine lines and wrinkles. Vitamin C products are typically used as part of a multi-step skincare regimen, so consider pairing this serum with an anti-aging cream–perhaps one with hyaluronic or an exfoliating acid–from this list.

To buy: $11; amazon.com .

amazon.com

11 RoC Retinol Correxion Deep Wrinkle Night Cream

With retinol at the forefront of this anti-aging night cream, getting rid of wrinkles even deep ones to achieve smoother skin is the main goal. The formula is designed to work through the night as you sleep and should be applied to your face and neck every night either as the final step of your skincare routine or before a moisturizer. Positive reviewers note not only how effective this wrinkle cream is for promoting a more youthful complexion, but also how far just a little can go, with the product lasting for months. One shopper wrote, “I usually use it morning and night. I'm a middle-aged female and can definitely see a decrease in lines on my forehead, around my eyes and sides of my mouth. I've bought a ton of products on Amazon and this is my first review of a product because it's a face cream that actually works for wrinkles, for me.”

To buy: $17; amazon.com .

amazon.com

12 Besito Anti-Aging Neck Firming Cream

Once you’ve got your anti-aging routine for your face sorted, it’s time to turn your attention to other areas that can be quick to divulge our years, like hands, chest, and neck . Besito’s Neck Firming Cream may have a more modest number of reviews than other anti-aging products on this list, but with 89 percent of those ratings being positive, the wrinkle warrior has managed to get the attention of shoppers. While you can use most face moisturizers, serums, and creams on your neck and chest as well, this skincare product is specifically formulated with the crepey neck skin that can accompany age in mind. With an advanced, peptide complex, vitamin E, shea butter, and fruit extracts, the formula helps tighten, firm, lift, and repair the neck and decollete areas. Happy customers love the “silky feel,” how soft it makes their skin (one reviewer’s husband even commented), and how noticeable the results are in as little as two applications.

To buy: $17; amazon.com .

Amazon.com

13 DRMTLGY Anti-Aging Tinted Moisturizer With SPF 45

If you want to streamline or shorten your daily routines, opt for anti-aging products with multiple functions beyond diminishing wrinkles, fine lines, and dark spots. DRMTLGY's tinted moisturizer is one of Amazon's best-selling in the category, and it does much more than just enhancing your skin's appearance immediately with a dose of moisture and a natural-looking layer of coverage. The lightweight formula also works to improve your skin's hydration and overall texture over time with soothing, skin-nourishing ingredients niacinamide (also called B3), hyaluronic acid, and vitamin E. The cream even does preventative anti-wrinkle work by blocking UVA and UVB rays with broad spectrum SPF 45 via the active ingredient zinc oxide. If you don't think this anti-aging product lives up to its promises, you have 60 days to get a full refund on your purchase.

To buy: $20; amazon.com .

Amazon.com

14 Bella Jade Makeup Setting Spray With Organic Green Tea

For a quick rejuvenating spritz of skincare goodness, pick up this makeup setting spray infused with herbal extracts, green tea, and more anti-aging ingredients. You can use the product on your fresh makeup to keep it in place and also throughout the day to treat any dryness or tightness as you experience it. Over 2,200 shoppers have given this multi-tasker a perfect five-star rating, with one writing, "Being a makeup artist of 20 years, I have never found a refreshing, enlightening, smell-good setting spray in probably 15 years. This spray by Bella is absolutely remarkable, because the results are astonishing. It's as if you are 'gluing' your finished makeup job sealed-shut for HOURS on end. But of course, there's no feeling to it such as sticky or tacky. It's actually addicting!"

To buy: $12; amazon.com .
